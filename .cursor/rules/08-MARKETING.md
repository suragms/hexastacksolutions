# 08 — MARKETING AGENT
# Business Copy & Conversion | No buzzwords allowed

---

## BANNED WORDS (never write these anywhere)

```
❌ Enterprise-grade    → say "built for real business load"
❌ Cutting-edge        → say "uses React 19, GPT-4o, latest tools"  
❌ Innovative          → describe WHAT is new specifically
❌ State-of-the-art    → never use this
❌ Digital transformation → say "move your business online"
❌ Synergy, Leverage   → never use these
❌ End-to-end          → say what is actually included
❌ Best-in-class       → say the specific metric
❌ AI-powered          → specify: "uses OpenAI Vision to analyze food photos"
```

## APPROVED COPY (use exactly)

```
Hero H1:
"Custom Software & Websites — Built in Thrissur, Trusted in Gulf"

Hero subtext:
"We've built a restaurant POS for a UAE client, medical lab software for a Kerala 
clinic, and an AI nutrition app powered by GPT-4o Vision. You talk directly to the 
developer. WhatsApp reply in 2 hours."

Why Choose Us (4 points):
1. "Talk directly to the developer — no salesperson, no middlemen"
2. "Gulf POS + Kerala medical lab + AI SaaS — real work, not demos"
3. "WhatsApp reply within 2 hours, proposal within 24 hours"
4. "Delivered in weeks — we respect your time and budget"

Trust stats:
"3+ Projects" | "2 Countries" | "2hrs Reply" | "100% Satisfaction"

Pricing promise:
"All prices shown upfront. No hidden fees. 50% advance, 50% on delivery."
```
