# 18 — MARKETING MANAGER AGENT
# Positioning, Messaging & Campaign Alignment

---

## READ BEFORE DECIDING MESSAGING OR PRIORITIES

Use when deciding what to say where (home vs services vs pricing) or prioritising business/marketing tasks. Align with 20-day plan and 21-STARTUP-AGGRESSIVE for sequence.

---

## POSITION STATEMENT (use in copy and about)

"We are a 2-person development team from Vatanappally, Thrissur. We have built a restaurant POS used in the UAE, a medical lab system used in Kerala, and an AI nutrition app powered by GPT-4o Vision. You talk directly to the developer. We reply on WhatsApp within 2 hours. No agency fluff. Real software. Real results."

---

## MESSAGING HIERARCHY (page flow)

1. **Hero** — Headline + subtext with Thrissur/Gulf and proof (POS, lab, NutriScan); primary CTA WhatsApp + secondary "Get quote"
2. **Proof** — 3 real projects with location badges (UAE, Kerala, SaaS)
3. **Trust** — Stats (3+ projects, 2 countries, 2hrs reply, 100%); testimonials if available
4. **CTA** — Pricing hint ("Starting from Rs.15,000"), WhatsApp again, contact form

---

## CAMPAIGN BEATS (20-day / 90-day)

- **Week 1–2**: WhatsApp outreach (5/day); fix site (hero, portfolio, WhatsApp green); GBP + Search Console
- **Week 3–4**: LinkedIn case-study posts; Kerala/Gulf groups; follow-up on enquiries
- **Month 2**: Referral program; more blog + LinkedIn; first paid clients
- **Month 3+**: SEO kicks in; content engine; Gulf push

---

## WHEN TO USE

- Choosing hero, services, or pricing copy
- Deciding order of sections or CTAs on a page
- Planning what to do next (business side) with 21-STARTUP-AGGRESSIVE
