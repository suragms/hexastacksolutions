# 14 — PSYCHOLOGICAL COPY AGENT
# Words That Convert | Trust & Risk Reversal

---

## READ BEFORE WRITING CUSTOMER-FACING TEXT

Use with 08-MARKETING (banned/approved lists) and 13-CUSTOMER-BEHAVIOUR (audience). This agent focuses on **psychological triggers**: specificity, risk reversal, and pain-point language.

---

## BANNED (same as 08-MARKETING — never use)

```
Enterprise-grade, Cutting-edge, Innovative, State-of-the-art
Digital transformation, Synergy, Leverage, End-to-end, Best-in-class
"AI-powered" without specifying (e.g. "GPT-4o Vision to analyze food photos")
```

---

## PHRASES THAT RESONATE (use these)

- **Speed / reliability**: "Replies in 2 hours", "Proposal within 24 hours", "Delivered in 2–3 weeks"
- **Trust / access**: "No middlemen — you talk directly to the developer", "Free consultation"
- **Money / risk**: "50% advance, 50% on delivery", "All prices shown upfront", "We fix it free if you’re not satisfied"
- **Proof**: "We built software for a restaurant in UAE", "Medical lab software for a Kerala clinic", "GPT-4o Vision" (specific tech, not "AI")

---

## PRINCIPLES

1. **Specificity over vague**: "GPT-4o Vision" not "AI-powered"; "2 hours" not "fast reply"
2. **Risk reversal**: Reduce perceived risk (free consultation, satisfaction promise, clear payment split)
3. **Pain-point language**: Mirror what they say ("developer disappeared", "website no one finds")
4. **Numbers**: "3+ projects", "2 countries", "2hrs reply", "100% satisfaction"

---

## WHEN TO USE

- Any customer-facing copy: hero, CTAs, forms, services, pricing, testimonials
- WhatsApp pre-fill messages and email templates
- Blog and LinkedIn post openings (speak to pain, then solution)
