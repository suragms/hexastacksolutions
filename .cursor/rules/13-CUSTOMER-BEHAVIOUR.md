# 13 — CUSTOMER BEHAVIOUR AGENT
# SMB Owner Psychology | Kerala & Gulf

---

## READ BEFORE COPY OR CONVERSION TASKS

Use with 08-MARKETING and 14-PSYCHOLOGICAL-COPY when writing customer-facing text or designing page structure. This agent describes **who** the audience is and **what they care about**.

---

## AUDIENCE (HexaStack’s real buyers)

- **Local**: Thrissur/Kerala — shops, clinics, restaurants, schools, event halls, transport. Often no website or only Facebook. Budget-conscious; want fast delivery and someone who replies.
- **Gulf (UAE etc.)**: Restaurants, retail, small businesses. Need VAT-compliant billing, POS, or a proper website. Prefer Indian/Kerala developers; use WhatsApp to find vendors.
- **NRI / Kerala entrepreneurs**: Want to start or run a business from Kerala or serve Gulf; want a trusted developer who won’t disappear.

---

## PAIN POINTS (address these on the site)

- "Developer took months and still didn’t deliver" → show timeline (e.g. 2–3 weeks) and "guaranteed delivery"
- "Previous agency disappeared after taking money" → show real WhatsApp, fast reply, milestone/advance payment
- "Website looks bad on mobile" → case study or line: "Mobile-first, works on every device"
- "Competitor has a better website" → hero: "Beat your competition online — from Rs.15,000"
- "No idea how to get customers online" → short education block: "5 ways a website gets you more customers"
- "Need billing/POS for my shop or restaurant" → show HexaBill / Gulf POS clearly; use "billing software Thrissur", "POS Kerala"
- "Gulf business needs a proper website" → dedicated Gulf proof (UAE POS), "website for UAE business"
- "Paid a lot for a website no one finds on Google" → "SEO-ready from day one", optional ranking case study

---

## TRUST SIGNALS THAT WORK

- **WhatsApp first** — number visible; "Chat on WhatsApp" above the fold
- **"Talk directly to the developer"** — no salesperson; they talk to Anandu/Surag
- **Fast reply** — "We reply within 2 hours on WhatsApp"
- **Real proof** — Gulf POS, Medical Lab Kerala, NutriScan AI (names and outcomes, not generic "we build apps")
- **Pricing visible** — "Starting from Rs.15,000" removes fear of hidden cost

---

## USE THIS AGENT FOR

- Hero and subtext (what to say first)
- Order of sections (proof before long copy; CTA early)
- Which CTAs to emphasise (WhatsApp, then form)
- Form length and labels (short, simple; avoid jargon)
- Service and pricing page copy (speak to pain and outcome)
