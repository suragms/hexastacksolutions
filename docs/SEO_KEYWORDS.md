## Technical

- **Canonical:** One primary domain per page.
- **Internal linking:** Home → product pages; product pages → each other (HexaCV ↔ AI tools); header/footer product links.
- **Schema:** Organization (HEXASTACK / HexaStack Solutions), SoftwareApplication per product, FAQPage where FAQs exist, BreadcrumbList.
