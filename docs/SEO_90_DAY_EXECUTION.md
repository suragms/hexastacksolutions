# SEO 90-Day Execution Roadmap

## Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2): Data + quick wins

- Upload Ahrefs exports into `docs/seo-ahrefs-inputs/`.
- Run `npm run seo:ahrefs` and validate:
  - `keyword_master.csv`
  - `top30_priority_keywords.csv`
  - `quick_wins_positions_11_40.csv`
- Apply quick wins on money pages (`/`, `/services`, `/work`, `/products/hexabill`):
  - title/meta refinement
  - H1 opening paragraph alignment
  - primary CTA consistency
  - internal links from FAQ and proof blocks

## Phase 2 (Weeks 3-6): Priority page expansion

- Execute top-30 keyword-to-page mapping from `docs/SEO_PAGE_CLUSTER_MAP.md`.
- Publish 4-6 support posts targeting unresolved pain points:
  - VAT compliance
  - POS reconciliation
  - Kerala website lead quality
  - Gulf rollout process
- Add proof snippets and case links on service pages.

## Phase 3 (Weeks 7-10): Template quality control

- Audit `/services/*` and `/seo/*` templates for uniqueness.
- Keep indexable pages with distinct value.
- Set weak/duplicate pages to `noindex` until enriched.
- Expand schema and internal linking from location pages to canonical service URLs.

## Phase 4 (Weeks 11-12): Review + second wave

- Re-run Ahrefs export and pipeline.
- Compare keyword deltas by market:
  - Thrissur
  - Kerala
  - Gulf
- Promote rising terms from quick-win list into primary clusters.
- De-prioritize low-fit keywords.

## KPI dashboard definition

Track weekly:

- Count of top-20 rankings for commercial terms by market.
- Clicks and impressions for money pages (GSC).
- Organic sessions to `/contact`, WhatsApp click-through rate.
- Number of keywords in positions `11-40` (quick-win pool size).
- Cannibalization incidents (same keyword mapped to multiple URLs).

## 4-Week Fast Ranking Sprint (Thrissur + Kerala + Gulf)

### Week 1

- Finalize tiered keyword list:
  - buyer intent
  - local low competition
  - Gulf commercial
  - high-volume long-term
- Update homepage title/H1/opening copy with 4-5 priority keywords.
- Baseline tracking setup for ranks/clicks/leads.

### Week 2

- Optimize `/services` for:
  - Thrissur service-intent terms
  - Kerala small-business terms
  - UAE/Qatar/Saudi service-intent variations
- Ensure one primary cluster per URL and no duplication.

### Week 3

- Strengthen `/work` with two proof-led keyword sections:
  - Kerala business website outcome
  - UAE/Gulf POS rollout outcome
- Add internal links from proof blocks to `/contact` and WhatsApp CTA.

### Week 4

- Align Google Business profile language with Thrissur/Kerala keyword set.
- Validate local entity consistency (name, phone, address, service areas).
- Re-check quick-win keyword movement in positions 11-40.
